>_Are you eligible?

Quick sanity check before you get too excited:

✓ You're in Group 14 — state number between #3114 and #3315
✓ You meet the power cap for the current event — not sure what it is? Ask in Discord, we don't bite
✓ It's been at least 25 days since your last transfer — no state-hopping

Three for three? Keep reading.

>_How it works

The transfer window runs for about a week in three phases. You mostly just need to know about Phase 2, but here's the full picture:

Phase 1
Pre-Transfer · 3 days
We're doing admin. Setting the power cap, figuring out who's coming in. If you've already chatted with an alliance leader, they're putting your name in the hat right now. Go touch some grass.
Phase 2
Invitational · 2 days
This is the one that matters. If an alliance has vouched for you, your invite lands here. When it does — leave your current alliance, sort out your Enlistment Office if needed, and accept. Don't leave it sitting. Invites have a window and we're not going to chase you.
Phase 3
Open Transfer · 2 days
No invite needed. Meet the power cap, spot's available, you're in. First come, first served. Set an alarm.
>_Getting an invite

Two flavours. Most people come in on an ordinary invite.

Most common

Ordinary invite

Find an alliance you like, talk to their R4 or R5, don't be weird about it. If they want you, they submit your name to the President. No vote, no tribunal, no essay — just meet the power cap and show up.

Not sure which alliance fits? Browse the alliances and find your people →

Top tier

Special invite

For the genuinely exceptional. Typically you'd need to be top 10 on both our Labyrinth leaderboard and our Hero Power leaderboard at the same time.

That said — special invites are handled case by case, not by checklist. Even if you're not quite in our top 10, reach out to an R4 or R5 anyway. We've made exceptions before. We'll do it again. Just make a good case for yourself.

Special invite requests go through a vote. It's a shared resource, so everyone gets a say.
>_Moving in as a group (10+)

Want to move your entire band of friends? We love the energy.

It's straightforward if you're upfront and willing to work with us on the logistics. Every alliance gets 5 invites per transfer event, so we'll work together to get everyone in as smoothly as possible. Some of your group will come in during Phase 2, others via Phase 3 — and if your group is large, we'll work to get everyone across as quickly as we can.

It's not instant, but it works.

>_Transferring your entire alliance?

Now we're talking. Bigger lift, but the door is open.

Fair warning: an alliance transfer goes through a vote across all our alliances. Everyone needs to be on board — that's not bureaucracy, that's just how a healthy state works.

01
Show up early and introduce yourselves
Contact the President or an R4/R5 from any alliance. The transfer window is not the time to make a first impression.
02
Get settled in the meantime
TOO and RED are our SvS alliances. Create a farm there, get to know the state, and see how we operate. Think of them as your hotel while you scout apartments. No obligation, no pressure — just a good way to figure out if we're the right fit before you fully commit.
03
Have a look around
We prioritize filling our existing alliances first. Chances are some of your group fits somewhere already. Talk to people. Browse the alliances →
04
If the fit is real
And you genuinely see yourselves as part of this state long-term — reach out to the President via the state Discord → They'll bring it to governance.
05
Once approved — we commit fully
We'll sort the invites, find you a shell alliance, and help build your tech up. We don't half-commit.

The golden rule: reach out well before the transfer window. A last-minute alliance transfer request is a great way to stress everyone out, including yourself.
